Bedford Festival is a 'make-shift' Music and Arts Festival seeing out the end of 2020. Featuring famous International and Local Talent such as Mr. Ping, Det. Gunter and Dr. Flopper.

Bedford is a much loved and adored residence amoungst friends and the festival was held days before its inevitable goodbye.

Outcomes include 2x A2 Posters,Facebook Event Cover Photo and Music Graphics.
