Bedford Festival is a 'make-shift' Music and Arts Festival seeing out the end of 2020. Featuring famous International and Local Talent such as Mr. Ping, Det. Gunter and Dr. Flopper.
Bedford is a much loved and adored residence amoungst friends and family. The festival was held days before its goodbye.
Outcomes include 2x A2 Posters, Facebook Event Graphics and Music Graphics which were displayed on a projector.
