Sunny Press is a joint collaboration born from lockdown reflecting on changing relationships with nature throughout a period of isolation. The small press hosts a mixture of words and art from friends and family - hoping to encourage contributors to 'look on the bright side of life'.

Sunny takes on a sophisticated yet playful editorial design style, breaking conventional type boundaries through experimenting with engaging layouts. Sunny's pieces can often feel confronting but aim to give readers a new lease of life as we begin to enter a new COVID-Normal.
