2. What is your understanding of brand? What’s the difference between creating a brand and doing some really sexy design or TOV? How does your role fit into the overall project?
Brand describes all the tangible and non-tangible elements that make up a "thing". Poor use of words I know - But this means that a brand need to have congruence and consistency across all it's outputs. In the digital age, a brand isn't just logos, posters and business cards. It is the designer's role to translate and reintepret the otherwise invisible elements of a brand such as it's purpose, mission and personality across all platforms where audiences may interact with it.

Sexy design pieces look fantastic but they are fragile and brittle. Without considering a brand's intangible elements it is impossible for the designer to create something that is enduring, true to character and effective. My role is to create design that speaks to strategy in the most universal form possible.
