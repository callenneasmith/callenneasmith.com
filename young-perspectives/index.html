Young Perspectives

‘Young Perspectives’ is a writing platform designed for close friends to share their stories about life from the viewport of their 20s. Here, you’ll find a collection of personal pieces that reflect on our limited yet unequivocally compelling life experience.

Contributors are Melbourne based with various levels of writing ability. All photos are supplied by the authors. This platform is updated with new writers and work on an adhoc basis, serving as a time capsule, materialising a moment in time.
