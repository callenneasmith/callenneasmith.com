Bedford Pizzeria is a North Melbourne Pizzeria with an affinity for good food and mateship. Churning out Pizza from a secluded laneway in the Inner City.
Inspired by the Commune Group's New Quarter Vietnamese restaurant in Richmond, Bedford Pizzeria similarly manifests an edgy, brave visual style adapted around a number of abstract characters depicting the owners of the establishment.
Located in North Melbourne, Bedford seeks to target a younger demographic of adults who are likely to engage with the Pizzeria not only for its good food but the complimentary branter and charming nature of the restaurant. Hence, illustrations hint at a cheeky, silly and relatable character who likes to write their phone number on business cards for you to call again later for example. Bedford Pizzeria is hypothetical project.
